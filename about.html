About Enhancify

Overview

Enhancify is a groundbreaking app unlike no other facial enhancer. Utilizing optimized algorithms, Enhancify is able to reduce all physical imperfections digitally, allowing you to appear truly flawless. Need to impress that 10 on tindr? Simply use Enhancify and we guarantee people will swipe right on you. Enhancify has conducted large scale scientific study across the globe, returning one hundred percent success rate in attracting mates.

How It Works

Enhancify analyzes over four-hundred and twenty aspects of the human face, comparing them to vast databases of photoshopped celebrities to determine what changes need to be made to accomplish perfection. After initial analysis, Enhancify works pixel by pixel to carefully construct your image. Once the adjustments have been made, a proof checking bot analyzes the image to ensure that even the most minute imperfection is taken care of.

But Actually

This project was built at WildHacks, a 24 hour Hackathon at Northwestern University in November 2014. This project was built with the help of Bootstrap, Dropzone.js, and facial recognition from Jay Salvat.
